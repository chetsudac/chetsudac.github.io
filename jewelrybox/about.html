Personal Collection

"A Jewelry Box" is a data set that will introduce you to my personal jewelry collection that I have gathered over the years. Each piece in the box has its own story and background. Some were gifted to me by loved ones, while others were found during my travels or bought simply because I fell in love with the design. Some hold sentimental value, such as a ring passed down from my grandmother, while others remind me of a special occasion or time in my life.
